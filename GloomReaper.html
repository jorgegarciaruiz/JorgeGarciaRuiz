About

A series of creatures have invaded the Galiant Lands and going out at night is no longer an option. You are The Huntress, and your mission is to eliminate these creatures before it's too late. You were cursed and blessed with the powers of Blaze and Gloom, granted by your Scythe, you must confront these creatures that dwell in the shadows. Explore the floating islands and challenge anyone who stands in your hunt, unleash your power and emerge victorious. The game was developed by Smoky Tanuki Games in Unreal 4 as part of Master 's Degree in Video Game Development of the Universidad Complutense de Madrid, by a group of more than thirty people in different departments and supervised by professionals of the industry.
