JOAQUIN GRACIOSIN TRILOGY

Global Game Jam January 2024

About

Game developed in unreal in only 3 days in which we will incarnate a clown psychologist who will have to cure his patients by means of the laughter therapy. The game is made up of two parts, the first of which is a puzzle genre in which we will have to decide what type of humour our patient likes and choose the answers accordingly. The second part of the game is a 2.5D Beat 'em up with pixel art aesthetics in which dissatisfied patients will come to give us a beating in the parking lot where we will have to face them with three types of attacks / jokes and having to use the appropriate one.
