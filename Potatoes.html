About

This is my first game, it was developed in three days in unity, it is a local multiplayer Party Game, where we will have to face each turn to a mini-game and if we win we can move the millenary potato on a board, the objective is to move it to your territory avoiding the traps that both you and your opponents can put on the map.

What did i do?

In this project I was involved as a programmer and designer, and among the tasks I carried out were:

  • Game design
  • Programmer of one of the mini-games
  • Bug fixing
